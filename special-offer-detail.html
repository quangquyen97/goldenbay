Three Bedroom Villa Pool Beachfront

Experience more on your next visit! We will pick you up at the airport and whisk you along one of the most scenic coastal drives in the world. Enjoy our stylish accommodations and breakfast for 2 adults with a generous USD 200 credit every day. Enjoy at our restaurants, bars, or spa. Celebrate an unforgettable evening at La Maison 1888, sip a creative cocktail at L_o_n_g Bar, or melt away your cares in our hideaway spa. Every day, there’s more to enjoy!

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation for two adults including daily breakfast.
  • Daily USD 200 credit towards any outlet including La Maison 1888, Citron, Barefoot, Long Bar, In-room Dining and the Spa
  • Return airport transportation

Terms and Conditions

  • Booking window: 01 days in advance
  • Credit is not accumulative and expires nightly
  • Credit cannot be used for room charges, or towards Spa Retail products or BENSLEY Outsider Gallery
  • Cancellations made before 6:00 PM (local hotel time), 7 days prior to arrival will result in no charge. Cancellations made after that time will be assessed a one-night charge plus applicable taxes.
